Cool Cat: Kids Superhero

Cool Cat: Kids Superhero is a children's movie created to teach kids the fun of making friends, the danger of strangers, and other social skills useful for developing children. The main character, Cool Cat, is put in social situations that might not be the most fun for anyone, but gets out of them, showing kids how to act. Cool Cat: Kids Superhero covers bullying, stranger danger, friendship, schoolwork, what to do when you find a gun, and many more important skills all children need to thrive. For the low cost of just $12.99, you can get the DVD and start teaching your kid necessary life skills to set them up for success.

Who Made Cool Cat?

Derek Savage is a writer, producer, director, editor, host, actor, and costume designer for Derek Savage Productions. He has won the 2002, 2003 and 2004 Golden Web Awards, the World Web Award of Excellence, and has been nominated for many other awards. Derek savage aims to spread common sense among young children through his many movies and books.Derek Savage has also done many things outside of Cool Cat, such as Gun Safety for Women (And Men Too!)

Derek Savage hosts a talk show with Cool Cat
